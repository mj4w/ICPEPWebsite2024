    • Who We Are

    The Institute of Computer Engineers of the Philippines BulSU - Meneses Campus shall and developed capable students to become leaders and direct the objectives of the organization and as well uphold the ideals of the Filipino students.

    Formed in 2020, ICpEP.SE BulSU - Meneses Campus shall and has developed capable students to become leaders and direct the objectives of the organization and as well uphold the ideals of the Filipino students in attaining a national, scientific, mass-oriented and global competitive type of education.

    Vision, Mission and Goal

    ICPEP MISSION

    To become the leading online hub for computer engineering students, fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence in the field of technology through accessible resources, mentorship, and community engagement.

    ICPEP Vision

    To become the leading online hub for computer engineering students, fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence in the field of technology through accessible resources, mentorship, and community engagement.

    ICPEP GOAL
